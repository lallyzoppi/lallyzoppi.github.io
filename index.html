America's Cup Cagliari 2026

The Louis Vuitton 38th America's Cup Preliminary Regatta took place in Cagliari, Sardinia, from 21 to 24 May 2026, marking the beginning of the Road to Naples 2027. Inspired by AC40 racing conditions, this project explores sailing performance analytics using simulated racing telemetry, including high-speed foiling, strong winds, and tactical manoeuvres.

Cyclone Harry – Extreme Event in Sardinia

Cyclone Harry was a significant meteorological event affecting Sardinia between 19 and 22 January 2026, characterized by intense winds, extreme precipitation, and severe sea conditions. This project analyzes mean wind speed, maximum gusts, and their spatial distribution across the island.
