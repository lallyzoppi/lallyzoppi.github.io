Americas Cup Cagliari 2026

The Louis Vuitton 38th America’s Cup Preliminary Regatta took place in Sardinia, in the port city of Cagliari, from 21 to 24 May 2026, marking the start of the “Road to Naples 2027”. Inspired by the America’s Cup regatta conditions, we propose a Sailing Performance Analytics Project Using Simulated Racing Telemetry. The goal is to reproduce typical high-performance sailing scenarios, including strong wind conditions, high-speed foiling, and tactical manoeuvres similar to those experienced in AC40 fleet racing.

Cyclone Harry Extreme Event in Sardinia

Cyclone Harry represented a significant meteorological event affecting Sardinia between 19 and 22 January 2026, characterized by the combined occurrence of intense winds, extreme precipitation, and severe marine conditions. To gain a more comprehensive understanding of the wind conditions associated with Cyclone Harry, it is useful to examine both the mean wind speed and maximum wind gust distributions across Sardinia, as well as their spatial relationship.
